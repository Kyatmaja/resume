About

Kyatmaja Lookman holds a diverse professional portfolio, currently serving as a Karate Instructor and Managing Partner at Kamselindo and Partners Law Firm, both based in Jakarta. Additionally, he contributes to multiple companies in significant roles, being the CEO of PT. Lookman Djaja Karoseri and PT. Pancanaka Nusantara Jaya, alongside serving as a Commissioner at PT. LSP Ekosistem Multimoda Indonesia and PT. Gudang Bang Indonesia. his extensive leadership experience includes positions as CEO at PT. Lookman Djaja Land and PT. Lookman Djaja Logistics, which dates back to 2005. In the realm of organizations, he actively leads as Chairman of both the Cultural and Tourism Awareness Community and the Indonesian Security and Safety Association. Academically, He is pursuing a Master of Law at Pelita Harapan University and has previously completed a Doctorate in Management Technology, alongside other notable degrees, including an MBA from the University of Technology Sydney and a Bachelor of Commerce from the University of New South Wales.

Kyatmaja Lookman has obtained numerous certifications and training qualifications across various fields. Key achievements include 1st DAN in Kyokushin Karate, multiple certifications from BNSP, such as Customs Expert, Instructor, and Auditor of Security Management Systems. Additional noteworthy training includes Investigation Training, Environment Assessment Impact Training, and Certified Mediator credentials from the University of Indonesia. Furthermore, he has participated in various leadership, security, and supply chain management programs, highlighting a commitment to professional development from 2010 to 2024.

Kyatmaja Lookman has received numerous accolades recognizing his academic and professional achievements. Notably, he was the Second-Best Graduate of the Law Study Program at Pelita Harapan University, achieving a GPA of 3.92, and the Best Graduate in the Accounting Professional Education Program at Atma Jaya Catholic University, with a perfect GPA of 4.00. he has also received multiple honors in the logistics sector, including the Bisnis Indonesia Logistics Award and the Indonesia Logistics Awards for Trucking Company of the Year in both 2023 and 2024. Additionally, recognition for his support during the Cianjur earthquake and various academic distinctions reflect a commitment to excellence in both his studies and professional endeavors. Throughout his career, he has received several awards related to transportation and logistics, affirming his significant contributions to the industry and community.
